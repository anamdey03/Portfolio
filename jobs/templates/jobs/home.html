About Sagarika Paul

I am nothing more than a girl next door. A software engineer by profession, an avid reader by habit. But was born with an insatiable love for travel, arts and literature. History and architecture intrigues me,so does the varied cultures and languages of the world. Who wants to work in 8 hours shift in an ac cubicle. But that’s the only way I get to fund my travel. I am here to share ALL aspects of my travel – from the amazing pictures and stories that will make you want to book flights for yourself.
